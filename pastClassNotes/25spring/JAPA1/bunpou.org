* Verbs
** Irregular Ferbs
  - There are only two irregular verb する ＆ くる, and they both transform differently.
  - Here are the transformations of these two verb with some tense and their negative form:
| する              | Short Form | Long Form    |
|------------------+------------+--------------|
| Present positive | する        | します        |
| Present negative | しない      | しません       |
| Past positive    | した        | しました       |
| Past negative    | しなかった   | しませんでした |
|------------------+------------+--------------|
|------------------+------------+--------------|
| くる              | Short Form | Long Form    |
|------------------+------------+--------------|
| Present positive | くる        | きます        |
| Present negative | きない      | きません       |
| Past postive     | きた        | きました       |
| Past negative    | きなかった   | きませんでした |
  
** Ru-verbs
*** Identification
  - Ru-verbs all ends in /e/ or /i/ with the suffix /ru/, so /eru/ or /iru/, no exceptions(for now, japanese is weird, there probably will be some exceptions while you learn further)
    the verbs ends in /uru/ are normally not /Ru-verbs/
*** Transformations
  - The transformation from Short to Long form for Ru-verbs is
    1. take the る away
    2. add ます at the end
  - Here are some examples:
| Short Form | Long Form |
|------------+-----------|
| ねる        | ねます     |
| みる        | みます     |
| おきる      | おきます    |
| たべる      | たべます    |

** U-verbs
*** Identification (yet to write)
*** Transformations
  - The transformation from Short to Long form for U-verbs is
    1. take the ending "u" sound away
    2. add "i" sound
    3. add ます at the end
  - Here are some examples:
| Short Form | Long Form |
|------------+-----------|
| はなす      | はなします  |
| のむ        | のみます    |
| いく        | いきます    |
| かえる      | かえります  |

** Negative Form
- For all verbs (that i know of) transform the same way, here are the steps
  1. take off the ます
  2. add a ません
  Here are some examples:
| Positive form | Negative form |
| します         | しません        |
| きます         | きません        |
| ねます         | ねません        |
| みます         | みません        |
That's about enough, you get the point.
