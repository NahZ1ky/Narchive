*quantum numbers in 3d
** n: principle quantum number
  n = 1, 2, 3, ..., n
  the physical significance of n, Energy state can be described by it, see function energyBn
** l: orbital angular momentum quantum number
  l = 0, 1, 2, ..., n-1
  vec{L} = L_x hat{i} +  L_y hat{j} +  L_z hat{k}
  the physical significance of l, l is the materal swirl of atom, calssically: vec{L} = vec{r} \times vec{p}, but in quantum, L = hbar \cdot sqrt{l(l+1)}
** m_l: z-component of orbital angular momentum quatum number
  m_l = -l, -l+1, -l+2, ..., l
  the physical significance of m_l, gets you the L_z hat{k} part of the vec{L}, L_z = m_l \cdot hbar
