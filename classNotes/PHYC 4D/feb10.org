* What is \(\psi\)?
the probability to discover particle between x and x + dx
\( \mathrm{prob}(x, x+dx) = \psi*(x) \cdot \psi(x) \mathrm{d}x \), so \( 1 = \int_{\mathrm{all possible places}} \psi*\psi \mathrm{d}x\)
Intensity reflects Probability
use this to make prediction for outcomes of experiments
